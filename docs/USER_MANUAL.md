## Introduction

Welcome to the Church Database Management System (CDMS). This system helps you manage your church's members, attendance, finances, events, and more in one place.

### System Requirements
- **Web Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Mobile:** iOS 14+ or Android 10+
- **Internet Connection:** Required

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Open your web browser and navigate to your church's CDMS URL
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **Sign in**

### First-Time Login
If this is your first time logging in:
1. Use the temporary credentials provided by your administrator
2. You will be prompted to change your password
3. Complete your profile information

## Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of your church's data:

### Dashboard Widgets
- **Total Members:** Count of all active members
- **Attendance Today:** Number of members present in today's service
- **Total Income:** Combined donations, tithes, and offerings for the current month
- **Prayer Requests:** Number of pending prayer requests

### Charts
- **Monthly Finances:** Bar chart showing income vs expenses over the last 6 months
- **Attendance Trends:** Line chart showing daily attendance patterns

## Member Management

### Viewing Members
1. Click **Members** in the sidebar
2. Browse the list or use the search bar to find specific members
3. Click on a member's name to view their profile

### Adding a New Member
1. Navigate to **Members**
2. Click **Add Member**
3. Fill in the required information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Gender
4. Add optional information (address, date of birth, etc.)
5. Click **Save**

### Editing a Member
1. Find the member in the list
2. Click the **Edit** icon
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

### Searching for Members
1. Use the search bar at the top of the members list
2. Search by name, email, or phone number
3. Results will filter as you type

## Attendance

### Recording Attendance
1. Navigate to **Attendance**
2. Select the service date
3. Select the service type (Sunday Service, Wednesday Service, etc.)
4. Check the box next to each member who is present
5. Click **Save Attendance**

### Viewing Attendance History
1. Navigate to **Attendance**
2. Use the date picker to select a date range
3. View attendance records for that period

### Attendance Reports
1. Navigate to **Reports** > **Attendance**
2. Select the date range
3. Click **Generate Report**
4. Download as PDF or Excel

---

## Finance Management

### Recording Donations
1. Navigate to **Finance** > **Donations**
2. Click **Record Donation**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Member (optional for anonymous donations)
   - Amount
   - Category (General, Building Fund, Missions, etc.)
   - Payment Method
4. Click **Save**

### Recording Tithes
1. Navigate to **Finance** > **Tithes**
2. Click **Record Tithe**
3. Select the member
4. Enter the amount
5. Select payment method
6. Click **Save**

### Recording Offerings
1. Navigate to **Finance** > **Offerings**
2. Click **Record Offering**
3. Select the service date and type
4. Enter the total offering amount
5. Click **Save**

### Recording Expenses
1. Navigate to **Finance** > **Expenses**
2. Click **Record Expense**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Category (Utilities, Maintenance, Supplies, etc.)
   - Amount
   - Description
   - Payment Method
4. Click **Save**

### Viewing Financial Reports
1. Navigate to **Reports** > **Financial**
2. Select the date range
3. Click **Generate Report**
4. Download as PDF or Excel

## Events

### Creating an Event
1. Navigate to **Events**
2. Click **Create Event**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Date and Time
   - Location
   - Recurring (if applicable)
4. Click **Save**

### Editing an Event
1. Find the event in the list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

### Deleting an Event
1. Find the event in the list
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion

---

## Announcements

### Creating an Announcement
1. Navigate to **Announcements**
2. Click **Create Announcement**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Title
   - Content
   - Publish Date
   - Expiry Date (optional)
4. Toggle **Published** to make it visible to members
5. Click **Save**

### Managing Announcements
- **Published:** Visible to all members
- **Draft:** Only visible to administrators

---

## Prayer Requests

### Submitting a Prayer Request
1. Navigate to **Prayer Requests**
2. Click **Submit Request**
3. Enter the title and description
4. Choose whether to submit anonymously
5. Click **Submit**

### Managing Prayer Requests (Leaders Only)
1. Navigate to **Prayer Requests**
2. Review pending requests
3. Click **Mark as In Progress** when prayer begins
4. Click **Mark as Answered** when prayer is answered

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Log In
- Verify your email address is correct
- Check that Caps Lock is not enabled
- Contact your administrator if you've forgotten your password

### Data Not Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache

### Mobile App Issues
- Ensure you have the latest app version
- Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings
- Restart the app
